LSST and TiDES

I am involved in the design of the Time-Domain Extragalactic Survey \citep[TiDES, ][]{2019Msngr.175...58S}, planned on the multi-object spectrograph 4MOST \citep{2019Msngr.175....3D}. Currently, TiDES is the only survey planned to perform systematic spectroscopic follow-up of LSST transients and transients' hosts. I've run some forecasts on how SN Ia cosmological constraints will look like using data from LSST and TiDES.

SN Cosmology with Euclid

I tested how combining LSST and Euclid data will improve SN Ia cosmology. We generated mock catalogues of NIR light curves from Euclid and combined them with simulated optical light curves from LSST. Check out our paper to see how this synergy between optical and NIR SN surveys will help us answering some of the most pressing questions of our field, e.g., whether/why SNe Ia are better standard candles in the NIR and what is the role of dust in SN Ia standardization.
